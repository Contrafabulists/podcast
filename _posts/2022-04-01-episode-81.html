Welcome to the 81st episode of the Contrafabulists podcast, Kin Lane and Audrey Watters' irregular discussion of the tall tales told about technology.

In this episode, we talk about healthcare, and specifically FemTech, or investments in the healthcare space that are targeting women--exploring the motivations behind why we invest in technology, what really matters amidst the technology, business, and politics of how technology is unleashed on the world.
